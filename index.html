About


Señor Kiko is a Montreal DJ from Mexico City. DJ Señor Kiko presents new electronic Latin sounds exploring a wide range of rhythms like Tropical Bass, Electro Cumbia and Moombahton. Kiko is the resident DJ of 'The Native ART•CULTURE evenings' organized by The First Nations Circle of UQAM, (Le Cercle des Premières Nations de l'UQAM).
